Process scan and connection requests from all devices (i.e., the White List is not in use)

◆ ADV_WHITE_LIST_FOR_ALL

#define ADV_WHITE_LIST_FOR_ALL   (0x03)

Process scan and connection requests only from devices in the White List.

◆ ADV_WHITE_LIST_FOR_ONLY_CONN

Process scan requests from all devices and only connection requests from devices that are in the White List

◆ ADV_WHITE_LIST_FOR_ONLY_SCAN

#define ADV_WHITE_LIST_FOR_ONLY_SCAN   (0x01)

Process connection requests from all devices and only scan requests from devices that are in the White List

◆ SCAN_ACCEPT_ALL

#define SCAN_ACCEPT_ALL   (0x00)

Accept all advertising and scan response PDUs except directed advertising PDUs not addressed to this device

◆ SCAN_ACCEPT_ALL_N_USE_RESOLVING_LIST

#define SCAN_ACCEPT_ALL_N_USE_RESOLVING_LIST   (0x02)

Accept all advertising and scan response PDUs except directed advertising PDUs where the initiator’s identity address does not address this device.

Note: Directed advertising PDUs where the initiator’s address is a resolvable private address that cannot be resolved are also accepted.

◆ SCAN_ACCEPT_WHITE_LIST_ONLY

#define SCAN_ACCEPT_WHITE_LIST_ONLY   (0x01)

Accept only advertising and scan response PDUs from devices where the advertiser’s address is in the White List. Directed advertising PDUs which are not addressed to this device shall be ignored

◆ SCAN_ACCEPT_WHITE_LIST_ONLY_N_USE_RESOLVING_LIST

#define SCAN_ACCEPT_WHITE_LIST_ONLY_N_USE_RESOLVING_LIST   (0x03)

Accept all advertising and scan response PDUs except: @liadvertising and scan response PDUs where the advertiser’s identity address is not in the White List; and

  • directed advertising PDUs where the initiator’s identity address does not address this device

Note: Directed advertising PDUs where the initiator’s address is a resolvable private address that cannot be resolved are also accepted.
