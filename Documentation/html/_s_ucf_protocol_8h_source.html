45 /*
46  * UCF extended format supports the following commands:
47  * - MEMS_UCF_OP_READ: read the register at the location specified by the
48  * "address" field ("data" field is ignored
49  * - MEMS_UCF_OP_WRITE: write the value specified by the "data" field at the
50  * location specified by the "address" field
51  * - MEMS_UCF_OP_DELAY: wait the number of milliseconds specified by the "data"
52  * field ("address" field is ignored)
53  * - MEMS_UCF_OP_POLL_SET: poll the register at the location specified by the
54  * "address" field until all the bits identified by the mask specified by the
55  * "data" field are set to 1
56  * - MEMS_UCF_OP_POLL_RESET: poll the register at the location specified by the
57  * "address" field until all the bits identified by the mask specified by the
58  * "data" field are reset to 0
59  */
